About TeleArena

TeleArena Cellular Services is a telecom engineering and testing organization specializing in LTE, 5G NR and IoT technologies.


We provide end-to-end device testing, feature validation, protocol testing, automation framework development, network troubleshooting, drive testing and RF survey solutions for telecom operators, technology partners and device manufacturers.

Core Services

📱 End-to-End Device Testing

Validation of LTE, VoLTE, VoWiFi, 5G NR and IoT devices.

🧪 Feature Testing

Verification of modem, network and interoperability features.

🤖 Automation Framework Development

Network simulator control, modem control and automated reporting solutions.

📡 LTE & 5G NR Protocol Testing

NAS, RRC, PDCP, RLC and MAC protocol validation.

🔍 LTE / NR Call Flow Analysis

Protocol-level troubleshooting and root cause analysis.

🚗 Field Test & Drive Test

Coverage validation and network performance assessment.

📶 RF Survey & Optimization

Coverage planning, signal quality analysis and optimization.

🌐 IoT Testing

NB-IoT and LTE-M validation and interoperability testing.
