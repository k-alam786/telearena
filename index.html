About TeleArena

TeleArena Cellular Services is a telecom engineering and testing organization specializing in LTE, 5G NR and IoT technologies. We provide end-to-end device validation, protocol testing, automation framework development and network troubleshooting services for telecom operators, technology companies and device vendors.

Core Services

End-to-End Device Testing

Comprehensive validation of LTE, VoLTE, VoWiFi, 5G NR and IoT devices.

Feature Testing

Validation of modem and network features, interoperability testing and regression verification.

Automation Framework Development

Custom frameworks for Network Simulator Control, Modem Control, Test Automation and Reporting.

LTE & 5G NR Protocol Testing

NAS, RRC, PDCP, RLC, MAC layer validation and protocol analysis.

Call Flow Log Analysis

Deep analysis of LTE and NR call flows, failures, mobility issues and throughput degradation.

Field Test & Drive Test

Coverage validation, mobility testing, benchmarking and network performance analysis.

RF Survey

Coverage assessment, signal quality analysis and optimization recommendations.

IoT Testing

NB-IoT and LTE-M validation, interoperability and connectivity testing.
